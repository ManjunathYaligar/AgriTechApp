About Target Agro Chemical

        Target is a E-shopping platform for poor farmers, which is the first E-shop in pune region to launch complete agro-products and services. We endeavour to provide agri-services by giving farmers quality products at minimum reasonable cost. We provide trusted and secured online shopping environment for product services related to agriculture, vast selection, low prices, fast and reliable delivery. It serves as a platform for the farmers to get tools and pesticides easily, quickly and at efficient rate. We are looking for new companies and innovation which will help farmers and boost their growth.
